Chapter 1

General Predictions (General Kandam)

This foundational chapter provides a holistic overview of your life's journey, key events, and the core characteristics that shape your destiny.

The General Kandam acts as a summary of your entire Nadi reading. It reveals major milestones, personality traits, and the overall direction your life will take, as inscribed on your unique palm leaf.

Chapter 2

Wealth, Family & Education

Gain detailed insights about your family background, educational path, communication skills, and financial prospects.

Discover how your family dynamics have shaped you, the educational opportunities available to you, and your potential for wealth. This chapter explores how your early environment influences your financial future.

Chapter 3

Siblings & Relationships

Understand the dynamics with your siblings, relationships with relatives, and the nature of your social connections.

This chapter reveals the bond you share with your siblings, including potential for support or conflicts. It also sheds light on your relationships with extended family and how these connections impact your personal growth.

Chapter 4

Mother, Assets & Property

Receive details about your mother's influence, ancestral property, vehicles, and other fixed assets.

Explore the significant role your mother plays in your life. This Kandam provides insights about ancestral property you may inherit, and offers guidance on acquiring real estate and vehicles that will bring prosperity.

Chapter 5

Children, Progeny & Creativity

This chapter offers predictions about your children, your creative talents, and artistic abilities.

Learn about the future of your children, including their well-being and destinies. It also uncovers your own creative potential and guides you on nurturing artistic talents for personal and professional fulfillment.

Chapter 6

Health, Diseases & Challenges

Get crucial insights into potential health issues, debts, litigation, and effective remedies to overcome them.

Discover potential health challenges you may face and the right time to take precautions. This chapter provides guidance on remedies and preventive measures to maintain well-being and successfully navigate life's obstacles.

Chapter 7

Marriage & Partnerships

Find detailed information about your marriage, the timing of the union, and the nature of your life partner.

This chapter provides deep insights into your marital life, including the characteristics of your spouse and the harmony of the relationship. It offers guidance on building a strong partnership that supports your life's purpose.

Chapter 8

Longevity & Unexpected Events

Receive predictions concerning your lifespan, potential accidents, and other unforeseen circumstances.

This chapter offers a rare glimpse into your longevity and prepares you for unexpected events. It provides wisdom on how to navigate sudden challenges and mitigate dangers that may arise in your life's journey.

Chapter 9

Father, Spirituality & Fortune

Gain insights into your relationship with your father, your spiritual growth, luck, and overall fortune.

This chapter reveals your spiritual path, your connection with your father, and the role of fortune in your life. It guides you on how to align with cosmic energies to attract abundance and spiritual fulfillment.

Chapter 10

Career, Profession & Business

Receive clear guidance on your ideal career path, professional achievements, and potential business ventures.

Discover your professional strengths, the most suitable career path, and the timing for important advancements. This chapter provides crucial insights into business ventures that are destined to bring you success.

Chapter 11

Financial Gains & Profits

Get specific predictions about wealth accumulation, sources of income, and overall financial prosperity.

This chapter reveals your financial destiny, highlighting potential sources of income and profitable opportunities. It provides strategic guidance on investments and financial decisions to maximize wealth accumulation.

Chapter 12

Expenditures, Losses & Foreign Connections

Learn about potential expenses, losses, foreign connections, and next birth.

Explore your patterns of expenditure and potential financial challenges. This chapter offers wisdom on how to manage resources effectively to avoid losses, navigate foreign connections, and understand the karma leading to your next life.

Chapter 13

Past Life Karma & Remedies (Shanthi Kandam)

Uncover karmic patterns from previous lifetimes that are affecting your current life and learn the prescribed remedies.

The Shanthi Kandam is crucial for understanding the root cause of current challenges. It reveals your past life karma and provides specific, powerful remedies to resolve these karmic imprints and improve your present life.

Chapter 14

Secret Knowledge & Mantras (Deeksha Kandam)

Receive knowledge of sacred mantras and yantras for protection against negative forces.

This chapter provides access to powerful, personalized mantras and information on yantras that can serve as a spiritual shield, protecting you from evil eyes and negative energies, ensuring your spiritual safety.

Chapter 15

Medicinal Remedies (Aushadha Kandam)

Guidance on herbal and medicinal remedies for chronic or complex health ailments.

This chapter delves into the ancient science of Siddha medicine, providing guidance on specific herbal remedies that can help in healing long-standing health issues mentioned in the 6th chapter.

Chapter 16

Political Life & Public Influence (Dasa Bhukti Kandam)

Predictions related to political power, public life, and the outcomes of major life periods (Dasa Bhuktis).

This final chapter provides a detailed analysis of your Dasa Bhuktis (planetary periods), predicting shifts in your public influence, and potential for success in politics or positions of authority.
